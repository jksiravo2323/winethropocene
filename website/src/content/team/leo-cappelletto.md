Leo Cappelletto is the CEO and head of research at Winethropocene. An Italian sommelier and wine consultant working between Italy and the UK, he holds the WSET Level 4 Diploma (DipWSET) and a master's in wine marketing and management. He spent twelve years at Petersham Nurseries and has been head sommelier at Soif, the London wine bar of the natural-wine importer Les Caves de Pyrène. He specialises in low-intervention wine and the growers adapting it to a warming climate — much of the Atlas's Italian-language reporting, producer access, and source leads starts with him.
